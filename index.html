About Us

Our story, mission & values

Yogesh Gupta, CPA (Ontario), CA (India)

I'm Yogesh Gupta, Founder and Principal of Dravin Solutions. Over the past 15 years I've built my career at the intersection of banking, corporate finance and real estate—beginning in investment banking at ICICI Bank, leading structured-lending at Edelweiss, and then serving as Deputy CFO at Country Garden Holdings (India) and CFO at Clarion Properties (Satya Group).

I hold dual professional credentials (CPA Ontario and ICAI Chartered Accountant), am pursuing my CBV designation through the CBV Institute, and am working toward CPA Canada's In-Depth Tax Program. Since founding Dravin Solutions, I've focused on delivering hands-on, senior-level advisory that bridges Canadian rigor with India's dynamic markets.

Leadership Experience:

Deputy Chief Financial Officer – Country Garden Holdings (2017-2021)
Chief Financial Officer – Clarion Properties, Satya Group (2021-Present)
Structured Finance Director – Edelweiss Financial Services

My mission is simple: translate complexity into clarity, empower entrepreneurs and established businesses alike, and build a trusted partnership that spans continents.
