About YKG Professional Corporation

YKG Professional Corporation (YKG PC) is a Canadian accounting and advisory firm founded by Yogesh Gupta, CPA (Ontario), CA (India). We serve Canadian entrepreneurs, professionals, and families with end-to-end accounting, tax, and advisory services, from routine compliance to strategic planning and valuation.

In addition, we offer specialized expertise for clients with India–Canada cross-border interests—bridging jurisdictions for tax planning, remittances, business structuring, and financial advisory.

With over 15 years of experience in banking, corporate finance, project lending, and senior leadership, Yogesh has:

  • Closed 400M+ CAD in debt, equity, and real estate transactions
  • Managed complex treasury, IFRS reporting, and compliance functions
  • Delivered solutions that combine Canadian rigor with global insight

Career Highlights:

Chief Financial Officer – Clarion Properties
Deputy CFO – Country Garden Holdings (India)
Structured Finance Leadership – Edelweiss Financial, Yes Bank, L&T Infrastructure
Investment Banking & Credit – ICICI Bank

Since relocating to Canada in 2022, Yogesh has earned his CPA Ontario designation, is enrolled in the CBV Program, and is pursuing CPA Canada’s In-Depth Tax Program to further strengthen valuation and tax expertise.

Our Logo Story

Centered in our signature deep-navy word-mark, two graceful arcs—one in sunset-orange and the other in coral-red—span overhead. The lower red arc roots us in resilience, integrity and the solid foundation forged through decades of banking-to-CFO leadership.

The upper orange arc reaches skyward, embodying ambition, creativity and the entrepreneurial spark we ignite for every client. Where they overlap, they form a protective canopy—an emblem of partnership and guidance, symbolizing the bridge we build between Canada's rigorous standards and India's dynamic markets.
